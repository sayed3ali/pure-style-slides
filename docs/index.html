Architecture

Six-phase pipeline.

Every deck runs through a structured workflow — from understanding your audience to automated quality checks. No phase is skippable.

0

Detect Mode

New deck from scratch, or redesign an existing .pptx? The skill identifies the workflow automatically.

1

Understand & Research

Parse objective, audience, timing. Then gather real data — academic papers, industry reports, government stats — and build a Source Registry with full citations.

2

Deck Blueprint

A slide-by-slide outline with layouts, visual direction, source references, and speaker notes. You approve before anything gets built.

3

Design & Generate

PptxGenJS rendering with theme colors, icon sprites, charts, source footnotes, and full presenter scripts. The layout sequencer ensures no two consecutive slides look the same.

4

Quality Assurance

Automated checks (contrast ratios, text overflow, slide count, speaker notes) plus visual inspection via PDF-to-image conversion.

5

Deliver

Polished .pptx file, optional handout PDF, and a speaker cheat sheet. Pitch decks can generate elevator + full variants from the same content.

Features

Everything a great deck needs.

Not a template library — a complete design system that thinks about typography, data, sources, accessibility, and narrative structure.

📐

Layout Sequencer

Programmatic layout variety. Alternates visual weight, prevents repeats, enforces the dark-light-dark sandwich structure.

📊

Data Visualization

Seven data-specific layouts (D1–D7), theme-matched chart styling, and insight-driven titles. Charts tell stories, not just show numbers.

🔬

Research Engine

Gathers academic papers, industry reports, government data. Builds a Source Registry that flows into footnotes, notes, and a References slide.

🎯

1,392 Vector Icons

Eight categories with dark + white variants. Fast keyword lookup for instant topic-to-icon resolution from sprite sheets.

🎤

Speaker Scripts

Every slide gets a full presenter script with talking points, transition hooks, source details, and anticipated Q&A prep.

🌍

RTL Support

Full Arabic, Hebrew, Persian support. Automatic layout mirroring, RTL-safe fonts, and bidirectional content handling.

Themes

Five curated palettes.
