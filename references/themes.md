# Theme Library

Curated color palettes and font pairings for Magazine-mode presentations. Each theme is designed for a specific mood and context. Choose the one that best matches your topic — never default to generic blue.

## How to Use

1. Pick a theme that matches the topic and audience
2. Use the primary color for backgrounds, section headers, and chart accents
3. Use secondary for supporting elements (cards, subtle backgrounds)
4. Use accent sparingly — callout numbers, icons, accent bars, highlights
5. Use the text colors specified for readability
6. Apply the font pairing consistently (header font for titles, body font for everything else)

## 1. Midnight Executive

The dark professional workhorse. Works for any corporate, finance, or consulting context.

```
PRIMARY:    1E2761  (deep navy)
SECONDARY:  CADCFC  (ice blue)
ACCENT:     4A90D9  (bright blue)
BG_DARK:    0F1638  (near-black navy)
BG_LIGHT:   F4F6FB  (off-white blue)
TEXT_DARK:   1A1A2E  (dark text)
TEXT_LIGHT:  FFFFFF  (white text)
TEXT_MUTED:  8B95A5  (muted gray-blue)
```

**Fonts**: Georgia (headers) + Calibri (body)

**Best for**: Finance, consulting, corporate, board presentations, quarterly reviews, strategy decks, status updates

## 2. Coral Energy

Warm and dynamic — brings energy without being unprofessional.

```
PRIMARY:    F96167  (coral red)
SECONDARY:  F9E795  (warm gold)
ACCENT:     2F3C7E  (deep navy)
BG_DARK:    2F3C7E  (deep navy)
BG_LIGHT:   FFF8F0  (warm cream)
TEXT_DARK:   2F3C7E  (navy)
TEXT_LIGHT:  FFFFFF  (white)
TEXT_MUTED:  9B8E8E  (dusty rose)
```

**Fonts**: Trebuchet MS (headers) + Calibri (body)

**Best for**: Marketing, events, consumer brands, startup pitches, product launches, training decks, conference talks

## Choosing the Right Theme

**By industry**:
- Finance / Consulting / Corporate → Midnight Executive
- Marketing / Consumer / Startups → Coral Energy
- Technology / SaaS / Healthcare → Ocean Gradient
- Investment / Real Estate / Luxury → Slate & Gold
- C-suite / Board → Midnight Executive or Pure Minimal (separate design system)
- Education / Training → Coral Energy or Ocean Gradient
- Islamic Finance / Halal → Slate & Gold or Ocean Gradient

**By audience**:
- C-suite / Board → Pure Minimal (separate) or Midnight Executive
- Investors → Slate & Gold
- Technical audience → Ocean Gradient
- General / Mixed → Coral Energy
- Creative / Design → Pure Minimal (separate)

**By tone**:
- Serious / Formal → Midnight Executive, Slate & Gold
- Energetic / Dynamic → Coral Energy
- Trustworthy / Calm → Ocean Gradient
- Premium / Luxurious → Slate & Gold
- Ultra-clean / Swiss → Pure Minimal (separate design system)
